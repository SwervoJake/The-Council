# Decision Log

*Records significant decisions made by the council for accountability and future reference.*

## Format

- **Date:** Decision date
- **Topic:** What was decided
- **Consensus:** Agreed outcome
- **Dissent:** Who disagreed and why (if any)
- **Rationale:** Key reasoning
- **Outcome (future):** To be filled when results are known

---

*Updated after each council session with approved decisions.*
